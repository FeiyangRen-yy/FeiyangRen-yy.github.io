Biography

I’m a first-year PhD student at the Mobility Science Lab, supervised by Dr. Roger Beecham and Prof. Ed Manley. My research focuses on social diffusion, especially how policy interventions shape people’s decisions and travel behaviour, and links with the INFUZE project. I aim to explore the dynamics of mobility transitions using computational and data-driven methods.

INFUZE

Inspiring Futures for Zero Carbon Mobility

Research Focus

Social diffusion and the role of the built environment in shaping travel behaviour and mobility transitions under policy interventions.

Methods & Tools

Network analysis, spatial modelling, and machine learning for urban and behavioural data.
